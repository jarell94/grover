# Community Guidelines for Grover

**Last Updated:** January 30, 2026

Welcome to Grover! Our community thrives when everyone feels safe, respected, and valued. These guidelines help maintain a positive environment for creators and users alike.

## Our Values

- **Authenticity:** Be yourself
- **Respect:** Treat others with kindness
- **Safety:** Protect yourself and others
- **Creativity:** Express yourself freely (within guidelines)
- **Inclusivity:** Welcome everyone

## Content Standards

### ‚úÖ What's Allowed

**Creative Content:**
- Original photos, videos, and art
- Vlogs and personal stories
- Educational content
- Entertainment and humor
- Products and services for sale
- Live streaming and performances

**Social Interaction:**
- Constructive comments and discussions
- Support and encouragement
- Sharing and reposting (with credit)
- Following and connecting
- Private messaging

**Marketplace:**
- Legitimate products and services
- Fair pricing and accurate descriptions
- Professional conduct

### ‚ùå What's Not Allowed

**Illegal Content:**
- Illegal activities or promotion thereof
- Drug sales or promotion
- Weapons sales
- Counterfeit goods
- Stolen property
- Pirated content

**Harmful Content:**
- Violence or threats of violence
- Self-harm or suicide content
- Child exploitation or endangerment
- Terrorism or extremism
- Dangerous stunts or challenges

**Hate and Harassment:**
- Hate speech based on race, religion, gender, sexuality, disability, etc.
- Bullying or harassment
- Doxxing (sharing private information)
- Impersonation or identity theft
- Targeted attacks or pile-ons

**Sexual Content:**
- Nudity or sexually explicit content
- Sexual solicitation
- Sexual content involving minors (zero tolerance)
- Non-consensual intimate imagery (revenge porn)

**Spam and Deception:**
- Spam, scams, or phishing
- Fake accounts or bots
- Misleading information
- Copyright or trademark infringement
- Malware or viruses

**Platform Manipulation:**
- Artificially inflating engagement
- Selling accounts or followers
- Evading bans with alternate accounts
- Hacking or exploiting system vulnerabilities

## Specific Feature Guidelines

### Posts and Comments

**Do:**
- Share original content
- Credit sources and collaborators
- Use content warnings for sensitive topics
- Report violations
- Engage constructively

**Don't:**
- Steal or repost without permission
- Spread misinformation
- Spam hashtags or mentions
- Engage in comment manipulation
- Harass in comments

### Live Streaming

**Do:**
- Follow all content guidelines
- Moderate your chat
- Respect copyright (music, video)
- Maintain appropriate content rating
- Be aware of who's on camera

**Don't:**
- Stream illegal activities
- Show graphic violence or sexual content
- Harass or attack others
- Stream copyrighted content without permission
- Show minors in inappropriate contexts

### Messaging

**Do:**
- Respect others' boundaries
- Block unwanted contacts
- Report harassment
- Keep conversations appropriate

**Don't:**
- Send unsolicited commercial messages
- Harass or threaten
- Share others' private information
- Engage in sexual solicitation
- Spam or send malware

### Marketplace

**Do:**
- Provide accurate product descriptions
- Honor your commitments
- Communicate professionally
- Resolve disputes amicably
- Report fraudulent sellers

**Don't:**
- Sell prohibited items
- Scam or defraud buyers
- Misrepresent products
- Fail to fulfill orders
- Engage in price gouging

## Age Requirements

- **18+:** Full access to all features
- **13-17:** Restricted access, parental consent required
- **Under 13:** Not permitted

**Age Verification:** May be required for certain features or if age is in question.

## Privacy and Safety

### Protect Yourself
- Don't share personal information publicly (address, phone, financial info)
- Use privacy settings to control who sees your content
- Be cautious about meeting online contacts in person
- Report suspicious behavior
- Block users who make you uncomfortable

### Protect Others
- Don't share others' private information without consent
- Respect others' privacy settings
- Don't screenshot and share private messages
- Report harmful content
- Support victims of harassment

## Reporting and Enforcement

### How to Report

**Report Content:**
1. Tap the "..." menu on any post or comment
2. Select "Report"
3. Choose violation type
4. Provide details (optional)

**Report Users:**
1. Go to user's profile
2. Tap "..." menu
3. Select "Report User"
4. Choose violation type

**Report in Live Streams:**
1. Tap report icon during stream
2. Choose violation type
3. Stream may be immediately reviewed

**Report Messages:**
1. Long-press the message
2. Select "Report"
3. Choose violation type

### What Happens Next

1. **Review:** Our team reviews reports promptly
2. **Investigation:** We gather context and evidence
3. **Action:** Appropriate action is taken
4. **Notification:** Reporter notified of general outcome (privacy permitting)

**Response Time:**
- Critical reports (safety threats): Within 1 hour
- High priority: Within 24 hours
- Standard reports: Within 72 hours

### Enforcement Actions

**Content:**
- Warning label
- Content removal
- Appeal option provided

**Accounts:**
- Warning notice
- Temporary suspension (1-30 days)
- Permanent ban
- Appeal option provided (except for severe violations)

**Severity Factors:**
- Nature and severity of violation
- Impact on victims
- User's history
- Intent and context
- Legal requirements

### Appeals

If you believe we made a mistake:

1. **Submit Appeal:**
   - In-app: Notification ‚Üí Appeal
   - Email: appeals@grover.app
   - Include: username, violation details, explanation

2. **Review:**
   - Different moderator reviews case
   - Additional context considered
   - Decision typically within 5 business days

3. **Decision:**
   - Action upheld, modified, or reversed
   - Final decision communicated
   - Some decisions are final (severe violations)

## User Tools

### Blocking
- Block users from contacting you
- Blocked users can't see your content
- Blocking is private

### Muting
- Mute users without blocking
- You won't see their content
- They can still interact with you

### Privacy Settings
- Control who can:
  - See your posts
  - Comment on your posts
  - Message you
  - Tag you in posts
  - See your location
  - Find you in search

### Content Filters
- Filter sensitive content
- Hide specific keywords
- Control what you see
