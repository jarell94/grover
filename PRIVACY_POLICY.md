# Privacy Policy for Grover

**Last Updated:** January 30, 2026

## Introduction

Grover ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using Grover, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

- **Account Information:** Username, email address, password (encrypted), date of birth, phone number (optional)
- **Profile Information:** Display name, bio, profile picture, location (optional)
- **Content:** Posts, comments, messages, photos, videos, and other content you create or share
- **Payment Information:** PayPal email or payment method details (processed securely by PayPal)
- **Communications:** Messages you send to us or other users

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

When you use Grover, we automatically collect:

- **Device Information:** Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
- **Usage Information:** Features used, content viewed, interactions, time spent, search queries
- **Log Information:** IP address, browser type, access times, pages viewed, crashes, and system activity
- **Location Information:** Approximate location based on IP address; precise location if you grant permission
- **Camera and Photo Library:** Access when you choose to create content or upload media

### 3. Information from Third Parties

- **Social Media:** If you link social media accounts, we may receive profile information
- **Payment Processors:** Transaction details from PayPal
- **Analytics Providers:** Aggregated usage statistics from Sentry

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

1. **Providing Services**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Enable posting, messaging, and social features
   - Process payments and payouts
   - Provide customer support

2. **Improving Our Services**
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Develop new features
   - Personalize your experience
   - Optimize performance

3. **Safety and Security**
   - Detect and prevent fraud
   - Enforce Terms of Service
   - Moderate content
   - Protect against abuse

4. **Communications**
   - Send notifications about your account
   - Respond to your inquiries
   - Send marketing communications (with consent)

5. **Legal Compliance**
   - Comply with legal obligations
   - Respond to legal requests
   - Protect our rights and property

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services:

- **Account Data:** Until you delete your account
- **Posts and Content:** Until you or we delete it
- **Messages:** Stored indefinitely unless deleted
- **Logs and Analytics:** Retained for 90 days
- **Legal Requirements:** Longer if required by law

After account deletion, some information may remain in backups for up to 90 days before permanent deletion.

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption:** HTTPS for all data transmission; encryption at rest for sensitive data
- **Authentication:** Secure session tokens, optional biometric authentication
- **Access Controls:** Limited employee access to personal data
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 security monitoring and incident response
- **Regular Audits:** Security assessments and penetration testing

However, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children's Privacy

Grover is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

**Age Verification:** Users must be 18+ or have parental consent (13-17)

If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately. Parents who believe their child has provided information should contact us.
